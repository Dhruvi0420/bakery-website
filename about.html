About Us

Handmade, small-batch baking using simple, quality ingredients

Founded in 2015, The Rustic Whisk began as a cozy neighborhood kitchen. We believe the best treats are born from patience, good butter, and a love for sharing. Our team wakes up early every day to bring you fresh breads, cookies, pastries, and signature cakes.

We source locally whenever we can, avoid unnecessary additives, and bake in small batches to maintain quality. Whether youre celebrating a birthday, grabbing a morning loaf, or treating yourself to an afternoon cookie  were here to make your day a little sweeter.

  • Quality ingredients and time-honored methods
  • Freshly baked daily, with seasonal specialties
  • Friendly service and custom orders
